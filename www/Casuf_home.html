About

The goal of the Cape Agricultural Sector Unity Foundation (CASUF) is to work towards the optimal integration of agriculture in this province: locally, nationally, on the African continent and internationally. 

  Towards this end it has three main focus areas

  • Connecting the dots across the agri-landscape.
  • Fostering collaboration among all stakeholders.
  • Contributing to the well-being of all agri-stakeholders.
